Who Are We?


The Entrepreneurship Cell, CAET,PUSA also known as E-Cell CAET PUSA, is the primary entrepreneurship promoting body of the College of Agricultural Engineering and Technology,Pusa managed and run by the students with the help of faculty members of the institute. Under the mentorship of NEC program of E-CELL IIT Bombay. It organise initiatives like Business model competition, guest lecturer, offline workshop etc



Vision


The Entrepreneurship Cell, CAET, PUSA targets to help in the development of India's entrepreneurial ecosystem by enabling easy and efficient interaction between its major components spanning students, working professionals, aspiring and existing entrepreneurs, mentors, angel investors, venture capital firms and corporates through events like interactive sessions, competitions and conferences
