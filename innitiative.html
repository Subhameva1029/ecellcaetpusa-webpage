Orientation Program


The first successful programme of the E-cell was an ORIENTATION PROGRAM for everyone entrepreneurship which was held on september27, 2022 where we got an opportunity to interact with -
• Dr. Niraj Kumar (Dean & Professor, school of rural management, XIM, BHUBHNESHWAR)
• Mr. Atul Ujjawal (Co-founder at Troika Dairy Products PVT.LTD. and Ex vice President, YES BANKS)








Eureka


The second mass successful event was a business model competition (Eureka) which was held on October7, 2022. It is Asia’s largest business model competition or early stage start- up where various teams participated and pitched their business ideas and model. It was very difficult to select the best model because many models were newsworthy. Here first winner received an opportunity for the direct fast- tracked evaluation in eureka 2022 at IIT BOMBAY.








Biz-Q


It was an offline quiz competition which was organised on December,15 2022 to test the knowledge of the audience and to encourage the students to establish a relationship between theory and learned concepts. The competition was started with a prelims round in which 5 teams were selected for further 3 rounds(Stage 1,Pass round, Rapid Fire round) and finally team with maximum points won the competition.








Mock IPL Auction


It was a mock IPL Auction organised on December, 21 2022 to let the people understand that just like cricket it’s not always a win win situation . The event started with a quiz competition with 20 teams out of which 9 teams were selected to participate in the auction. Each team was given an amount of 80 cr. To invest and buy at least 6players. Every player was assigned with some points. Finally the team with maximum points won the competition.








Webinar


It was an online workshop on scope of AGRICULTURAL BUSINESS by URBAN BIOTIX which is an start-up to promote modern agriculture and produce micro farmers in urban areas. Our speaker Mr. Aryan kumawat influenced everyone with his magical words. We learned the skills like problem solving, teamwork, empathy as well as learning to accept failure as a part of the growth process.








Illuminate


It is a series of offline workshop ( a complete guide to start promote and scale your venture) based on entrepreneurship jointly organised by E-cell IIT Bombay and E-Cell CAET Pusa. Our guest speaker Mr. Divyansh Raj who is a start-up enthusiast and start-up mentor at IIT Bombay, greeted everyone with a big smile and influenced everyone with his inspiring and magical words. Here maximum number of people received an opportunity to experience every aspects of entrepreneurship.














It was the event which made all of us proud of what we were doing. We could felt the entrepreneurial spirit which we all anticipated from the very start of this e-cell. With this we went on arranging many more programmes which up to some extend did encouraged the youth. Lastly there is not much left to write about E-cell CAET pusa other than the fact that they are doing a really good work and I hope that they keep on organising programmes in future to keep the entrepreneurial spirit active in the youngsters.
